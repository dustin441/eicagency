# EIC Agency MOF Static Creative Set

## Audience
Warm agency owners and decision-makers who visited the white-label paid media page or watched EIC prospecting creative.

## Strategy
Move from TOF category introduction to MOF proof and objection handling. Use only real EIC people and actual EIC dashboard imagery.

## Shared visual system
- 1080x1080 square Meta placements
- EIC navy gradient, cyan accent, white typography
- Existing EIC logo and visual identity
- Clear feed-size headline, one supporting statement, one CTA
- No AI-generated people or synthetic dashboard mockups

## Concepts

### 01. Reporting proof
**Job:** Make the service tangible.
**Headline:** Give clients reporting they can actually understand.
**Proof:** Actual EIC reporting dashboard.
**CTA:** See the system behind the service.

### 02. Real delivery team
**Job:** Prove delivery depth without an internal hire.
**Headline:** Meet the paid media department behind your agency.
**Proof:** Real EIC team portraits and named delivery functions.
**CTA:** Meet your behind-the-scenes team.

### 03. Client control
**Job:** Address the fear of losing the client relationship or brand.
**Headline:** Still your clients. Still your brand. Now with a paid media team.
**Proof:** Real EIC founders and explicit behind-the-scenes positioning.
**CTA:** See how white-label delivery works.

## Test recommendation
Run as separate ads in the same warm retargeting ad set with consistent body copy and landing page. Judge first on qualified landing-page views and booked fit checks, not CTR alone.
